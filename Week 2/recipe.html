Steamed Sausages with Vegetables


Description

Sweet or spicy sausages toping a colorful bed of various vegetables.


Ingredients

  • 1/4 Zuchinni
  • 1 Small carrot
  • 1/2 Small Onion
  • 1/4 Tomato
  • 2 Asparagus
  • 2 Mushroom
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 Sausages
  • 2 tbsp Water
  • Salt

Recipe

  1. Cut all vegetables into strips and all to steamer with water, oil, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Steam vegetables for 3 minutes and 30 seconds.
  3. Remove sausages from casing and cut into small bite size pieces.
  4. Once the timer is up place sausages into the steamer with the vegetables.
  5. Steam again for another 2 minutes and 30 seconds.
  6. Plate and enjoy.
