Sweet or Spicy Sausage with Vegetables Recipe

Ingredients:
1/4 Zuchinni
1 Small Carrot
1/2 Small Onion
1/4 Tomato
2 Asparagus
2 Mushrooms
1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Salt
2 Sausages
2 tbsp Water

Description:
A bed of vegetables of difirent colors and tastes toped with sweet or spicy sausages(your choice).

Instructions:
Cut all vegetables into strips and all to steamer with water, oil, and a pinch of salt.
Steam vegetables for 3 minutes and 30 seconds.
Remove sausages from casing and cut into small bite size pieces.
Once the timer is up place sausages into the steamer with the vegetables.
Steam again for another 2 minutes and 30 seconds.
Plate and enjoy.
